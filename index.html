Who i am

I am a creative User Experience and Interface Designer

I worked in the print and media industry in Germany and the startup ecosystem in Hong Kong. As a UI / UX designer, I take pride in ensuring that user needs take center stage. I graduated in Print & Media Technology and wrote my Bachelor’s Thesis about the optimization of usability for industrial B2B websites.

Prior to that, I was the Design & Marketing Manager at Steel Available and worked as a Freelancer to help startups with branding, prototyping and everything around user experience. Currently, I am exploring the blockchain industry and support HEX as a UI / UX Designer and Product Manager.
